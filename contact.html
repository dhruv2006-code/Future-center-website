FAQ

Common Questions

What are the admission requirements? +
Anyone can join our courses! No prior computer experience is required for Basic Computer or Graphic Design courses. Web Design requires basic computer knowledge.
Do you offer part-time or weekend batches? +
Yes! We offer morning, afternoon, and evening batches on weekdays, plus dedicated weekend batches for working professionals.
Is the certificate recognized? +
Yes. Our certificates are government-recognized and accepted by companies across India and abroad.
Do you provide placement assistance? +
Absolutely. We have a dedicated placement cell that helps graduates with job readiness, resume building, and interview preparation.
Can I pay course fees in installments? +
Yes! We offer flexible payment options including 2-installment plans. Contact us for details.
